Introducing AWS UG Berlin

Learn, Meet, Inspire

Our community gladly welcomes everyone

Navigating the world of cloud computing can be overwhelming, which is why we are here for you. As the official AWS community in Berlin, we organize events for over ten years, providing a friendly and inspiring space for learning and growth. Our gatherings cover a wide range of topics, from AWS native solutions to DevOps, Data, Development, and more.
We are proud to have a supportive community of nearly 5,500 members who share their knowledge and experiences with one another. We are grateful for the support of our distinguished sponsors, who help make our events possible. If you're interested in partnering with us for one of our future sessions, we'd be happy to hear from you and work together to create a meaningful and inspiring event.
